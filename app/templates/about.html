Welcome


It’s no surprise that with the amount of information at our disposal also brings a large amount of groupthink, what others think is important, valuable, and how to attain it. Because of this, it’s becoming increasingly important to me to have a continued strong commitment to self-reflection - to have a meaningful life, you need to decide for yourself what is valuable and meaningful for you.
